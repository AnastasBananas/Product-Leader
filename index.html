01
Flagship · Founder & CPO

Neirochat — an AI platform I built 0→1, and the agents that now run it

A B2C AI SaaS — LLM router + creative-AI platform for Russian-speaking users and SME owners.

Role  Co-Founder & Chief Product Officer When  2026 – present Stack  Claude Code · Python · Yandex Direct/Metrica · Replicate · OpenRouter · SQL
The problem

Millions of Russian-speaking direct users and SME owners are locked out of the best AI tools by payment and access barriers. There's no Russian bank-card support, you need a VPN, and the tools are scattered across a dozen sites. These people want the output — portfolios, visuals, text, video, product cards, logos, promo copy — generated by top AI models.

What I did
  • Built the product 0→1 on a lean, AI-assisted stack — owned vision, discovery, build, GTM, monetization and the KPI system. Our small team ships daily, idea-to-deployed-feature in hours, using AI coding agents.
  • Live for 17 consecutive weekly build-measure-learn cycles with full-funnel KPI tracking: impressions → clicks → visits → registration → trial generation → paywall → purchase, plus weekly ROAS, CPA, ARPPU and cohort read-outs.
  • Redesigned monetization on cohort data — moved to pay-as-you-go packages + subscriptions, added a "subscribe & save" model, and built a per-package unit-economics model (margins ~22% to ~90% by package, with currency and utilization risk flagged).
  • Built AI agents to run the recurring ops so a 3-person team covers the ground of a much bigger one.
Results — early-stage, real numbers
  • Registration conversion +15% (15.1% → 17.4%) from a single H1/landing change — now reaching 30%.
  • Paywall-reach conversion lifted from 22% → 85% after redesigning paywall timing; a record 18.9% purchase-per-registration week followed.
  • CTR doubled (12% → 23%) through ad-creative iteration.
  • 94% of registrations run a trial; 6% convert to purchase.
  • Built the full monetization + margin model that now governs pricing — proven in ×3 ARPPU.
1aSearch-campaign management agent

What it does: a rules engine that watches the whole Yandex Direct account and flags exactly where money is leaking and where it's working. It runs across every level — keyword, ad group, campaign, search query and the account budget — grading each finding by severity (critical / warning / info), with a concrete suggested action attached.

Why it matters: the weekly read used to mean pulling numbers from three places by hand. Now the agent does it, grades what it finds, and proposes the fix — I approve or override. Because the targets are tied to actual payer LTV, it optimizes toward profit, not vanity CTR.

1bSEO content pipeline — 10-phase publishing agent

What it does: an end-to-end agent that turns a topic + competitor URLs into a live, SEO + GEO/AEO-optimized article: competitor analysis → SEO query research → content brief → branded HTML with Article + FAQPage schema → an "answer-first" block engineered for LLM citation (ChatGPT, Alice, Perplexity) → 3 on-brand images via Replicate → re-hosted on Yandex storage → HTML patched → published via the admin API.

Why it matters: one person publishes SEO- and LLM-optimized articles in ~10 minutes instead of the 6–7 hours of content-creator work. It's human-in-the-loop by design — nothing goes live without my sign-off.

SplitMetrics — freemium 0→1 to 8-figure revenue, plus an AI engine clients trust with real budgets

Official Apple Search Ads platform for mobile growth.

Role  Product Manager → Lead PM When  2022 – 2024 Scale  3 product teams · 20 people
The problem

Growth was constrained by a long, expensive enterprise sales cycle. A large SME segment wanted Apple Search Ads tooling but couldn't enter through enterprise sales — and the automation that existed wasn't trusted with live ad budgets.

What I did
  • Owned the pivot to self-serve freemium — defined the ICP and gating logic, re-architected onboarding to self-serve, launched a multi-channel GTM.
  • Took the AI-powered bid-optimization and rules engine from concept to market leadership — redefined the rule grammar, execution guarantees and failure-mode guardrails, and evolved it from rule-based logic to various models trained on live campaigns for CPA.
Results
  • 0 → 1,500+ SME clients and 8-figure revenue in 9 months; market-leading position.
  • +40% ARR over 18 months, with these products a core driver.
  • Automation engine: 15% client budget savings and 40% less manual work — trusted enough that clients let it act on their spend.
